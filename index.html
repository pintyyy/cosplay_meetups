üìå Meetup Leader Quick Guide

If you submit a meetup, you're the leader for the event (similarly to how panels are organized). The AM! team will do their best to schedule based on your availability

  • Tips:
  • Have some photo categories planned ahead, specific to your fandom
  • Arrive a few minutes early at the location. When it's time to start, introduce yourself! "Hi, my name is *your name* and I will be coordinating the *Insert Fandom here* Meetup today!"
  • Communicate there is a 1-hour time limit on the space, you will be going through some pre-selected photo categories, and there will be time at the end for more!
  • Be kind and include everyone!
